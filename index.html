WELCOME to "SHOW me the Stats"

A Sports Data Visualization Platform

Our Goal here at SHOW me the Stats (QB Edition) is to present your favorite Quarterback's seasonal data in a way that makes sense! Think about how many times have you gone to a player's stats page and seen data listed in tables, like the ones below. Now think about how long it takes you to find the stats your looking for and break it down or make calculations. It can take some time. This is where we come in. Whether it be percentages, or yards and TDs per game, or per opponent we desire to VISUALIZE this data and let your EYES do the thinking allowing you to spend more time strategizing.

In these examples we have provided tables of each QB's Data for reference.

Baker Mayfield

Season Breakdown

The Cleveland Brown's Baker Mayfield had a 2018 season was one for the record books. After not starting the first 3 games of the season he still managed to throw for 27 Touchdwons. That was the most by any rookie Quarterback since Peyton Manning or Russell Westbrook! He also managed to hold above a 50% Completion Percentage for 13 out of 14 games with 65.8% and a TD/INT Ratio of 1.92. Not bad. He was most definitely a crutial part the Browns completing a 7-8-1 season after a extremely disappointing 0-16 2017 regular season.

Blake Bortles

Season Breakdown

The Jacksonville Jaguars Blake Bortles was in his 4th NFL season in 2018 and it was a bit of a struggle compared to his 2017 season. Bortles and The Jags finished the 2017 season at the NFC Championships with a loss to the New England Patriots. The 2018 season ended with the Jags not making the playofs. Bortles finished 2018 with an average completion percentage of 60%, but in spite of this we see that for The 2018 season he finished with a TD/INT ratio of 1.18. YIKES!
